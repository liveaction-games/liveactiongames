Privacy Policy – Sudoku Daily / Live Action Games

Last updated: February 2025.

Who we are

Live Action Games is a small studio run by Kyle. We make puzzle and casual mobile games, including Sudoku Daily.

Data we collect

In-app data (stored on your device only)

  • Game progress, statistics, and preferences (e.g. difficulty, theme)
  • Purchase state for the "Go Ad-Free" subscription (stored locally to honor your choice)

This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Advertising (AdMob)

Sudoku Daily uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect data for ad serving and analytics. Google's privacy policy applies to that data: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

How we use data

We use in-app data to run the app (progress, settings) and to honor your "Go Ad-Free" purchase. We use AdMob to show ads when you have not purchased ad-free. We do not sell your personal data.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the in-app feedback or contact option in the app settings.
